Sandwich

Description

Homemade sandwich that makes you feel satisfied

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cooked chicken breasts, sliced

Steps

  1. Prepare the sandwich spread by mixing together the mayonnaise and blue cheese
  2. Spread 2 tablespoons on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Top 4 of the prepared pieces of bread
  4. serve with the remaining blue cheese spread on the side.
