Lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna that makes you feel satisfied

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is lightly browned and bubbling, about 30 minutes.
