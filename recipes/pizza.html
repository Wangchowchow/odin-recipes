Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza that makes you feel satisfied

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine water and yeast in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 minutes.
  2. Mix crushed tomatoes and pizza sauce together in a bowl to make sauce.
  3. Let pizza rise in a warm area until puffy, about 1 hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
